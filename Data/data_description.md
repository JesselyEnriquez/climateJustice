
The Socio - Economic, Physical, Housing, Eviction, and Risk (SEPHER) dataset was a compialation of four major outside sources:
- CDC Social Vulnerability Index
- FEMA National Risk Index 
- Home Mortgage Disclosure Act
- Evictions datasets
